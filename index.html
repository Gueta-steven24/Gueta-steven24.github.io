About Me

Frontend Developer

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Esteven Gueta—although I typically spell my first name without the letter 'E' at the front to encourage pronunciation as 'is-tee-ven'—a passionate Frontend Developer specializing in CSS and JavaScript. My focus is on designing and creating visually appealing websites.

I hold a Bachelor of Science degree in Information Technology from Sorsogon State University-Bulan Campus. Throughout my academic journey, I actively participated in Capstone research projects with my co-researchers, gaining valuable programming experience and honing my skills in frontend development using various programming languages.

My mission is to provide clients with satisfying designs that perfectly suit their websites. I am captivated by the interplay of colors, aesthetics, and animations, as these are my areas of expertise. With a range of services offered, including frontend development, graphic design, photography, and photo and video editing, I assist clients in achieving their goals and objectives.

Beyond work, I enjoy exercising and occasionally hitting the gym. In my free time, I find inspiration by watching professional programmers on YouTube and indulging in movies. These activities contribute to maintaining a well-rounded perspective, fueling my creativity in programming and nurturing my passion for filming and other digital endeavors.
